RS COACHING IS EVOLVING


Change is the law of nature, and to embrace,
adapt and master the change is the trait of the wise.
Being responsive to the changes in the society and to
the changes required in the mode of education, we
decided to come up with a new identity, drawing
important lessons from our 25 years of the wealth of knowledge.
This new identity that stands on the footstone of cherished
values and vision of our founding members will help us
reinforce our beliefs and prepare our students to
make their mark in future.
